about hack4fem

Our goal is to explore how engineering can be applied in the field of feminist activism. We aim to inspire new ideas and projects by showcasing the funny and creative sides of feminism.

Solid prototypes, games, apps that share experiences, improve understanding, empower individuals - your creativity is the limit! Read more about our mission.
